Tokyo can be overwhelming for first-time visitors, especially when you're trying to maintain your training routine. After spending three months exploring the city's fitness scene, I've narrowed down the absolute best spots for travelers who refuse to skip leg day.

Whether you're a powerlifter looking for serious equipment, a CrossFitter seeking a drop-in friendly box, or just someone who wants a clean gym with reliable basics, this guide has you covered.

What You Need to Know Before You Go

Training in Tokyo comes with a few quirks compared to Western gyms:

  • Shoe etiquette matters. Most gyms require you to change into indoor training shoes. Don't wear the same shoes you walked in with.
  • Tattoos can be an issue. While attitudes are changing, some gyms still have strict no-tattoo policies. Call ahead or be prepared to cover them.
  • English isn't guaranteed. Staff may not speak English, so having Google Translate ready helps. That said, gym culture is universal‚Äîpeople are friendly once you're there.
  • Day passes exist but vary. Some gyms offer tourist-friendly drop-ins; others require membership. I've noted which ones welcome travelers.

The Best Gyms in Tokyo

1. Gold's Gym Harajuku

If you're serious about lifting, this is your spot. Gold's Gym Harajuku is Tokyo's premier bodybuilding and powerlifting facility, packed with equipment most commercial gyms only dream of having.

What makes it great: Multiple squat racks, dedicated deadlift platforms, Olympic lifting area, and enough free weights to keep you busy for hours. The atmosphere is pure iron‚Äîno nonsense, just focused training.

Final Thoughts

Training in Tokyo doesn't have to be complicated. Whether you're dropping in for a single session or planning to train throughout your stay, these five gyms will keep your routine intact.

My personal recommendation? If you're serious about lifting, start with Gold's Gym Harajuku. If you want a full facility experience with saunas and pools, go with Tipness Roppongi. And if you just need something reliable and familiar, Anytime Fitness won't disappoint.
