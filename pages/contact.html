Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check in to a gym? +
Open the app, find the gym on the map or search for it, tap on the gym listing, and hit the "Check In" button. You'll earn a city stamp and points for each check-in!
How do passport stamps work? +
Every time you check in to a gym in a new city, you'll earn a passport stamp for that city. Collect stamps from cities around the world and track your fitness journey!
How do I add a gym that's missing? +
Tap the "Add Gym" button in the app, enter the gym's details including name, address, and equipment available. You'll earn points for contributing to the community!
Is LiftAway free to use? +
Yes! LiftAway is free to download and use. All core features including gym discovery, check-ins, reviews, and passport stamps are completely free.
Can I use LiftAway offline? +
You can view your saved favorites and passport stamps offline. However, searching for new gyms, checking in, and posting reviews require an internet connection.
How do I delete my account? +
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data, including reviews, check-ins, and passport stamps. This action cannot be undone.
