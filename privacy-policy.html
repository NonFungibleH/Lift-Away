Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2024

Introduction

LiftAway ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by 3uild LLC, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Location preferences

User-Generated Content

  • Gym reviews and ratings
  • Photos of gym facilities
  • Check-in history
  • Favorite gym lists
  • Comments and interactions with other users

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics and interactions
  • Check-in locations and timestamps
  • Search queries within the app

Location Information

  • Precise location data (when you use map features or check in to gyms)
  • Approximate location (for gym discovery near you)
  • City-level location for passport stamp collection

We only collect location data when you actively use location-based features. You can control location permissions through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service - Display gyms near you, enable check-ins, and manage your account
  • Personalize your experience - Show relevant gyms based on your location and preferences
  • Enable social features - Display your reviews, check-ins, and passport stamps
  • Improve our Service - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Communicate with you - Send account-related notifications and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure safety and security - Prevent fraud, abuse, and maintain platform integrity
  • Comply with legal obligations - Respond to legal requests and enforce our Terms of Service

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Other Users

  • Your public profile information (username, profile photo)
  • Reviews, ratings, and photos you post
  • Check-in locations and timestamps (if you choose to make them public)
  • Your passport stamp collection

With Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting services (for data storage)
  • Analytics providers (to understand app usage)
  • Customer support tools

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

For Legal Reasons

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of LiftAway, our users, or the public
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.

After account deletion, we may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or as required by law, such as:

  • Transaction records for financial compliance
  • Content that has been shared publicly (to maintain data integrity for other users)
  • Anonymous usage data for analytics

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View and update your account information in app settings
  • Download a copy of your data
  • Delete your account and associated data

Location Controls

  • Enable or disable location services through device settings
  • Choose whether check-ins are public or private
  • Control which location data is shared with the Service

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional emails
  • Manage push notification settings

Your Rights Under Applicable Laws

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA), including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")

LiftAway is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@liftaway.app.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure coding practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
